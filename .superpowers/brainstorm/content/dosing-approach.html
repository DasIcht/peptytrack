How should we approach Custom Dosing Frequencies?

When moving away from strict weekly/daily schedules, we need to balance clinical accuracy with flexibility.

A

Clinical Presets + "Split" Toggle

Users select standard clinical doses (e.g. 5mg weekly) and choose how to divide it (e.g., "Split into 2 doses per week"). The system automatically handles the math.

Pros: Foolproof, stays perfectly aligned with clinical trial step-ups.
Cons: Less flexible for users doing arbitrary schedules (e.g. every 5 days).
B

Arbitrary Frequency & Auto-Equivalence

Users input any frequency (e.g. "Every X days"). The titration wizard suggests the equivalent custom dose needed to maintain the target average steady-state circulation.

Pros: Maximum flexibility, accurately models exact custom schedules.
Cons: More complex UI, calculates specific doses that might not map cleanly to standard pen clicks or easy vial math.
